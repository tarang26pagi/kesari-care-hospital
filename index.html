About the concept

Designed from public hospital signals, not generic hospital templates.

This concept uses a custom saffron-and-teal palette inspired by the hospital's public local-brand presence, then pairs it with an editorial layout that feels trusted, human, and easy to navigate on both mobile and desktop.

The interface emphasizes the things local patients usually want fast: where the hospital is, who is available, which facilities are listed, and how to reach the team quickly.

Why this design works

Fast scanability, high-contrast calls to action, and strong reassurance blocks for patients and families.

What it reflects

Multispeciality care, practical accessibility, and a grounded community-hospital identity rather than a cold corporate feel.

Publishing posture

Static, lightweight, and ready for direct hosting on any standard web server without a build step.
